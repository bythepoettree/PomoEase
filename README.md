# 🍅 PomoFit

**Study hard. Break harder.**

PomoFit combines the Pomodoro technique with exercise. Study in focused
intervals, then follow an animated coach through a workout programme sized
to fill your break — so every study session keeps your body and brain sharp.

## Features

### Focus
- Pomodoro timer with presets (15/25/45/50 min) or a custom length (1–180 min)
- Task list with pomodoro estimates — tasks count up (2/4 🍅) as you complete
  full focus rounds and cross themselves out automatically when finished
- Session dots, auto-starting breaks, and a long break after every 4 rounds
- A bell rings and a browser notification fires when each timer ends, even
  if the tab is in the background

### Sweat breaks
- Four workout programmes (⚡ Cardio Spark, 🏋 Strength Circuit, 🧘 Core & Hold,
  🔥 Full Body) plus a 🎲 surprise mix
- Programmes are automatically sized to fill your remaining break time, with
  achievable rep targets and rests between exercises
- An animated coach demonstrates every move with realistic joint movement,
  and a trajectory strip shows exactly what's coming next

### Atmosphere
- Three generative soundscapes — Zen Bells, Rain & Birdsong, Ocean Waves —
  synthesized live in the browser, with a volume slider
- Rotating "Brain fuel" facts about exercise, hydration and cognition,
  flavoured with facts about your general area (city-level, IP-based)
- Hydration reminders every 45 minutes (can be switched off)
- Four themes: Forest (default), Ember, Ocean and Mono

### Profiles
- Create an account with a username and password to track focus rounds,
  minutes studied, total reps and recent activity
- Passwords are stored only as salted hashes; all data stays in your
  browser — nothing is sent to a server

## Use it

Open the live site — no install required, works on phone and desktop.
Press START to begin a focus round; pick a programme when the break arrives.

## Privacy

- Accounts, tasks and stats are stored locally in your browser only
- Location is looked up once per visit at city level (IP-based) purely to
  personalise the rotating facts; it is never stored or transmitted elsewhere
- Notifications are optional and requested only after you press START

## Limitations

- Accounts are per-device: signing in on a different device won't find
  your data (cross-device sync would require a backend)
- On iPhone, notifications require adding the site to your home screen
  (an iOS restriction)
